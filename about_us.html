About StockFlow

Revolutionizing inventory management for businesses of all sizes

Our Story

Founded in 2024, StockFlow began with 4 IT college students of University of San Carlos who recognized the challenges businesses face in managing their stock efficiently. What started as a simple tracking tool has evolved into a comprehensive inventory management platform serving thousands of businesses worldwide.

Today, StockFlow is recognized as a leader in inventory management solutions, helping businesses streamline operations, reduce costs, and make data-driven decisions.

Our Mission & Values

Mission

To empower businesses with intuitive, powerful inventory management tools that drive efficiency, reduce waste, and maximize profitability.

Vision

To become the global standard for inventory management solutions, transforming how businesses track, analyze, and optimize their stock.

Values

Innovation, Integrity, Customer Success, Continuous Improvement, and Collaborative Excellence guide everything we do.
