BUSINESS CONTINUITY & DISASTER RECOVERY

Business Continuity & Disaster Recovery (BCDR) refers to the strategies and processes that organizations put in place to ensure that critical business functions can continue during and after a disaster. Business continuity focuses on maintaining essential services and operations, while disaster recovery emphasizes the restoration of IT systems and data after a disruption. Together, BCDR plans help minimize downtime, mitigate risks, and ensure that businesses can quickly recover from unexpected events such as natural disasters, cyberattacks, or technical failures. Effective BCDR strategies include data backups, emergency response plans, and alternative operational solutions.
